About Us

Ceo Founder

As the Founder and CEO of KOW, [Erwin Smith] is driven by a deep passion for horology and design. With years of experience in the watch industry, [Erwin Smith] sought to create a brand that merges timeless craftsmanship with modern elegance. Under their leadership, KOW has grown into a symbol of quality and sophistication, appealing to those who value both style and precision. [Erwin Smith] is committed to ensuring that each KOW watch not only meets the highest standards but also resonates with the unique stories of its customers. Through innovation and a dedication to excellence, [Erwin Smith] continues to lead KOW toward becoming a global leader in luxury timepieces.

Members

The team at KOW is composed of talented individuals who share a passion for horology and design excellence. Each member brings their unique skills and expertise, from watchmaking and engineering to marketing and customer service, ensuring that every aspect of the brand reflects our commitment to quality. Together, the KOW team collaborates to create timepieces that not only meet but exceed our customers’ expectations. Our diverse team shares a common vision of crafting watches that tell a story, bringing elegance and precision to every wrist. At KOW, we believe that our collective dedication and craftsmanship make us more than just a brand – we are a family.

Quality of Watches

At KOW, we pride ourselves on the exceptional quality of our watches. Every timepiece is crafted with precision and attention to detail, ensuring superior performance and lasting durability. We use only the finest materials, such as high-grade stainless steel, premium leather, and advanced movements, guaranteeing that each watch stands the test of time. Our expert craftsmen and engineers meticulously inspect every component, ensuring flawless design and functionality. Whether it's the precision of the movement, the luxury of the finish, or the comfort of the wear, KOW watches are built to provide an unparalleled experience for our customers.
